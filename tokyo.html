Tokyo

Tokyo is a flirty and rebellious character, and she has a relationship with Rio, that stops and starts throughout the first heist. She shares a good friendship with Nairobi, the two having gotten drunk together many times. She doesn't like being told what to do and shows dislike for Berlin in the first series.

Tokyo is reckless and impulsive, she usually acts before she thinks, which sometimes results in things she regrets. She let her relationship with Rio complicate the plans for the heist.
